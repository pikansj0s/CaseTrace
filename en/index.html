How it works

Your folder structure is already your timesheet.

01

Add a root folder

Show CaseTrace where your client or case folders live (e.g. C:\Cases or C:\Clients). It finds the rest by itself.

02

Set a time budget per case

Assign hours and an hourly rate to a case. CaseTrace calculates costs automatically and alerts you at 25, 50, 75 and 100% of budget.

03

Work as you normally do

Open files, write, edit, read. CaseTrace logs passively at file and folder level. No buttons. No interruptions.

04

Report ready for invoicing

Export a case-specific report as PDF or CSV. Time spent, hourly rate, total — ready to attach to an invoice.

Statutory time registration

Danish law requires time registration.

Since 1 July 2024, every Danish employer must record employees' daily working hours — objectively, reliably and accessibly, kept for 5 years. CaseTrace captures your computer time passively as you work — an automatic starting point that you finalise and control yourself as your statutory working-time record, with no timers or manual note-taking.
